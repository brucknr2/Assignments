              9319318  Amir    Starting in the Fall of 1992, the weekly Metropolitan Atlanta 
              Theory Seminar (MATS) began its activity which this grant  continues to
              support.  The goal of the seminar is stimulating  activity in Theoretical
              Computer Science in the greater Atlanta  area.  Attendees include faculty and
              students from Georgia Tech  (the College of Computing, the Math Department,
              Industrial and  Systems Engineering, and Electrical Engineering); University of
               Georgia at Athens; Emory University; University of Alabama;  Dekalb College
              and two HBCU's, Spelman College and Clark Atanta  University.  ***
