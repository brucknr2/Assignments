Abstract    :
              9302162 Stern  The PIs have completed descriptions and interpretations of
              dredged, bathymetry and petrology of most of the Mariana Trough. In the present
              project they will carry out petrological/geochemical analysis of a selected
              group of samples. The data will be used to model evolution of incipient back
              arc basins. ***
