              9302865 Biscaye  Funds are provided so that the LDEO nephelometer data set
              collected over two decades from the worlds ocean can be complied into a usable
              form using current computers. The data will be plotted, interpreted and
              published. The PI has already amply proven the extreme value of this type of
              data in a completed study of the Atlantic in various oceanographic disciplines.
