Abstract    :
              In the past, this conference has                                               
              brought together academic,                                                     
              industrial, and government                                                     
              researchers with interests in the                                              
              principles and practices of                                                    
              biochemical engineering.  This                                                 
              conference series has served as a                                              
              mechanism to communicate important                                             
              developments and directions of                                                 
              significance to biochemical                                                    
              engineers.  Biochemical Engineering                                            
              VIII will continue this tradition                                              
              with sessions planned to update                                                
              ongoing areas of interest as well                                              
              as to highlight emerging areas.                                                
              Funds are to be used to partially                                              
              defray expenses for academic                                                   
              participants.
