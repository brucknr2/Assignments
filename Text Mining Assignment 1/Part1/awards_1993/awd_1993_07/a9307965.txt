Abstract    :
              The Eighth Conversation in Biomolecular Stereodynamics will be held            
              June 22-26, 1993 at the State University of New York, Albany. Young            
              and Senior Scientists, close to 500 people from 14-15 Nations, will            
              gather at SUNYA to discuss and debate the developing ideas and                 
              discoveries in:                                                                
                                                                                             
              RNA Structure and activity                                                     
              Parallel DNA's and RNA's                                                       
              Triplexes, Quadruplexes and Chromosomal Telomeres                              
              DNA Supercoils, Chromatin Structure & Accessibility                            
              Molecular Recognition                                                          
              Drug & Carcinogen-DNA Complexes                                                
              Molecular Mechanisms of Mutagenesis                                            
              DNA Structure & Function                                                       
              Protein Structure & Function                                                   
              %%%                                                                            
                                                                                             
              The sessions will consist of 68 lectures and about 250 poster                  
              discussions.  Of the 68 lectures, atleaset 28 of them will be                  
              delivered by outstanding women scientists.
