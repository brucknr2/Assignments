Abstract    :
              9316007 Kao   This award provides funds for equipment needed for plant and plant
              protoplast work.  Work will continue on biochemical characterization of
              structure and function of brome mosaic virus replication proteins and will
              begin on studies of the replication of the single-stranded DNA geminiviruses.  
              %%% Anticipated results from this research should advance our current
              understanding of the replication mechanism of plant viruses.  A better
              understanding of these processes will allow for better strategies in plant
              disease reduction as well as providing insight on the workings of other plant
              and animal viruses. ***
