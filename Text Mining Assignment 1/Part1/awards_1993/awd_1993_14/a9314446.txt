Abstract    :
              9314446     Wiens    This project will deploy a set of Ocean Bottom Seismometers
              across  the Lau Basin and Tonge Trench, to complement an already-funded 
              land-based array. The goal is to use seismic velocities to  interpret the
              structure of the mantle wedge and subducting slab,  and thereby to determine
              evidence for phase changes in the slab,  and sources of arc back-arc magmas.
