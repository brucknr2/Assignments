Abstract    :
              9314911  Smith    Our knowledge of the origins, history and distribution of the 
              chemical elements rests on measured elemental abundances in stars  and
              interstellar gas.  Key clues are found in the abundance  patterns of certain
              elements.  Detailed abundance analyses of the  heavy elements will be carried
              out for stars in globular clusters.   This yield crucial evidence on the early
              chemical evolution of the  galaxy and the time scale for the formation of the
              globular  clusters.
