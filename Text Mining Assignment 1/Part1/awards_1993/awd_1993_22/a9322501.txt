Abstract    :
               This project focuses on problems in two  interrelated  areas:  (1) algorithms
              for graph problems, and   (2) the design of data structures.  The common theme
              is the investigation of   efficient  computation in these areas.  The project
              is to generate improved    techniques, and characterize significant complexity 
              relationships.    A central issue is the efficient coordination of the
              acquisition of   information during a complex computational task.
