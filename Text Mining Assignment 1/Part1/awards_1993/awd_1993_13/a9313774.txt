Abstract    :
              This is a contract for various support services to be provided by              
              employees of National BioSystems, Inc. related to proposal                     
              handling, mailings to panelist and arrangements at panels meetings,            
              award and declination processing, and data entry before and after              
              the meetings of panels.  These activities are related to the                   
              NSF/DARPA initiative describe in NSF's Announcement No.  NSF-9319,             
              oriented towards "Research on Human Language Technology."  This is             
              not an annual program but the award is intended as a one-time                  
              support effort for the three-year NSF/DARPA initiative during a                
              period when regular staff support is dedicated to the processing of            
              standard and continuing grants and other on-going duties.
