Abstract    :
              9313625 Silver  This renewal award will complete processing  of seismic,
              sidescan, and other geophysical data collected on a cruise off New Guinea in
              1992. In addition to processing and analyzing the existing data set, the PI
              will do a gravity survey on nearby parts of the island in order to determine if
              the interpretations derived from the marine work are confirmed by the land
              data. Students from Papua New Guinea and Australia will be included in the
              work. ***
