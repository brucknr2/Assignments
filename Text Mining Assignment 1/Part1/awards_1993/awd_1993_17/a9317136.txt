              9317136  Krogstad       This award provides partial funding to support a
              technician  for the Department of Geology at the University of Maryland, 
              College Park. The technician will assume responsibility for the  operation and
              maintenance of the Department's mass spectrometry  laboratory used by students
              and faculty in a growing program of  basic research in watershed geochemistry,
              ore formation, and  origins of granites.  ***
