Abstract    :
              9317861  Thompson    This award provides funding for the first year of
              three-year  continuing award for support of an evaluator at Iowa State 
              University's Industry/University Cooperative Research Center for 
              Nondestructive Evaluation.  The Center continues to meet the  renewal criteria
              requirements of the I/UCRC Program.    The evaluator of the Center duties
              consist of writing a Historical  Profile, taking Surveys of industrial members
              and faculty, advising  the Director of the Center and IAB members, attending
              annual  evaluator meetings and conducting exit interviews with departing 
              companies.      The Program Manager recommends Iowa State University be awarded
               $8,000 for first year of three-year continuing award to support the  evaluator
              at the Center.
