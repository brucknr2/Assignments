Abstract    :
              9312330 Dwoskin The proposed symposium and follow on workshop will examine the
              forces shaping engineering research in academia in the US.  The goal of the
              project is to characterize the changing demands on academic engineering
              research (changes coming both from outside academia and from within) and to
              identify potentially effective responses to such changes on policymakers.  The
              symposim and workshop will be held in conjunction with the National Meeting of
              the National Academy of Engineering at the Academies' Beckman Center in Irvine,
              California in February 1994.  The symposium will be designed to allow the
              various involved parties to put forth their views.  The papers presented at the
              symposium and discussions a the workshop and the smaller, follow on workshop
              will be the primary basis for an assessmsnt by a committee of NAE members of
              the changing demands and possible responses.
