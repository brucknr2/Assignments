Abstract    :
                   Applicants to the NSF 1992 Minority Graduate Fellowship                   
              competition who were awarded "Honorable Mention" status and who                
              enrolled in a computer science or computer engineering graduate                
              program at a U.S. university were eligible to apply to the CISE                
              Directorate for this special award.  The purpose of the award is to            
              assist the student in both research and educational activities                 
              related to his/her graduate education.  The award is made on behalf            
              of the student to the institution with the student's advisor                   
              designated as principal investigator.
