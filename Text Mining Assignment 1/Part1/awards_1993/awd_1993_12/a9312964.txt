Abstract    :
              9312964   Moskowitz        This project will support conference on Enterprise
              Integration  to assess the state of the art, practice and direction of  
              information technology and management in organizations for the   purpose of
              establishing education and research agendas for their  integration into
              management and engineering programs.  The   conference will focus on showcasing
              leading-edge practices,   overviewing underlying technologies, examining the
              issues and   challenges enterprise integration poses, and assessing its  
              implications for education and research.        A select group of leading
              practitioners, academicians, and   other experts will participate in this two
              day conference.  A   conference proceedings will serve as a springboard to
              establish   educational, research, and implementation agendas for infusing  
              enterprise integration into university educational and research   programs.
