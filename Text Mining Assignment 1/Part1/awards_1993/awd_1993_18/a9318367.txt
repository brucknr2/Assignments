Abstract    :
              9318367  Tichy  Travel support is provided for a group of US tribologists to
              attend the International Symposium on Tribology in Beijing, China, during
              October of 1993 and to also make site visits to South Korean and Chinese
              research institutes.  The group will develop an assessment of the status of
              tribology research in the region and explore the opportunities for continued
              and expanded cooperative research activities.  ***      ~
