              9318316  Blumenschine    With National Science Foundation support Dr. Robert
              Blumenschine  will attend an international paeloanthropology conference to
              honor  Dr. Mary Leakey. The meeting will be held in Arusha Tanzania. In 
              Arusha, Dr. Blumenschine will also make arrangements for his  impending
              National Science Foundation supported archaeological  research project at
              Olduvai Gorge Tanzania. Both before and after  the conference Dr. Blumenschine
              will visit Dar Es Salaam to discuss  the research with Tanzanian scientific
              collaborators. He will also  talk to relevant Tanzanian officials in an attempt
              to obtain a  research permit.  ***
