              9318917  Seamands       This project connects Kirkwood Community College to
              NSFNET  through the Iowa Communications Network over a 56 kbps line.   Faculty
              and students will benefit from access to the resources of  the Internet,
              including libraries and supercomputers.  In addition,  they will be able to
              communicate and collaborate with colleagues at  other institutions in pursuit
              of research and educational projects.   The award provides partial support of
              the project for two years.
