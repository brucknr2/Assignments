Abstract    :
              9354084  Davidson    Under the leadership of Kerry Davidson (SSI and Teacher 
              Collaborative PI) this project will plan and implement a  conference on
              mathematics and science education in conjunction  with the states of Arkansas
              and Mississippi.  The conference  will be held during the second week of July,
              1993.  The tri-  state conference will focus on systemic change and highlight 
              exemplars from K-12, higher education, partnerships and policy  change from
              each of the states.  It is intended  as a regional  follow-up to the National
              Science Foundation Conference of  February 1993.***
