              9308195  Rusay       Diablo Valley College is establishing a connection to
              NSFNET  via a 56,000 bits per second line through the BARRnet mid-level 
              network.  The connection enables the faculties and students to  communicate and
              collaborate with the international scientific and  educational community, and
              to access network resources such as  libraries, databases and supercomputers. 
              This award funds part of  the costs for two years.  ***
