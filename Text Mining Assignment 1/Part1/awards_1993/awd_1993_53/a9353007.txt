Abstract    :
              9353007  Petry    The University of Oklahoma will initiate a 4-week,
              residential,  Young Scholars project in mathematics and the natural sciences 
              for 24 students entering the 12th grade.  This program represents  the second
              of two summer programs.  A learning summer" will  precede the YSP (funded by
              state monies).  In the YSP,  participants will be mentored by a University of
              Oklahoma faculty  member.  Participants will prepare both written and oral
              reports  on their research to be presented to their peers at the end of  the
              program.  Additional activities include career exploration  and ethics of
              science discussions.  ***
