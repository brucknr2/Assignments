Abstract    :
              9353867  Austin    The University of Maryland Baltimore County (UMBC) seeks to
              provide  a six-week summer residential, Young Scholars project with follow-  up
              during the academic year for 40 students entering grade 9. The  students will
              engage in classroom lectures and discussions, field  trips, and hands-on
              laboratory and research experiences in biology  and chemistry. The students
              will be involved in a research project  under the guidance of University of
              Maryland Baltimore County  faculty members serving as mentors. The program will
              also include  mathematics, computer, and technical writing instruction.
