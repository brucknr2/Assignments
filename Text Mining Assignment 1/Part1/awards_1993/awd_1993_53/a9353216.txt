Abstract    :
              9353216 Shuman  This Planning Grant will investigate the possibility of
              developing a national program in Teacher Enhancement for teachers in grades 7
              12 who teach environmental science.  The planning grant will study selection
              procedures for the participants, identify exemplary curriculum and recruit
              nationally recognized staff.  It will be held at the site of the National Youth
              Science Camp in West Virginia an ideal setting for environmental science
              education. ***
