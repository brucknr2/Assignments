Abstract    :
              9353856  Peploski    Clarkson University will continue its 5-week, residential,
              Summer  Research Apprenticeship Program in Science and Mathematics.  Students
              participate in a professional scientific research  experience.  Students work
              with a faculty mentor on a research  problem that is of current interest. 
              Lectures, workshops and  seminars as well as career exploration and ethics of
              science  discussions are also provided.  A multi-faceted program that  includes
              the participants high school teachers and alumni  participation complete the
              project activities.  ***
