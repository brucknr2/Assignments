              SSC-9353277  University of Toledo  Toledo, OH    Cooks, Helen, Collins,
              Laurence, McDonald, Sharon and Natsoulas,  Anthula    "The University of
              Toledo-PREP/TECH Program"    In the years 1993-35, a 5-week Prep/Tech Summer
              Institute Summer  Science Camp (SSC) and Academic Year Program will be held at
              The  University of Toledo and will be designed to; 1) afford fifty (50)  7-9
              grade African- and Hispanic- American students academic  enrichment
              opportunities in mathematics, science, and language arts  and to; 2) challenge
              and develop critical thinking abilities  through daily classroom exercises and
              laboratory projects which  employ a hands-on approach to problem solving; 3)
              expose students  to the laboratory environment through experiments that are
              designed  to increase their awareness and understanding of scientific and 
              technical methodologies; 4) familiarize students with computer  usage; 5)
              Provide stipends for students whose socio-economic  circumstances reflect a
              need for economic assistance to encourage  full participation in the Prep/Tech
              program; 6) Assist selected  students in their 9th grade transitional year with
              science and math  studies though an Extended Pre /Tech curriculum; 7) Provide
              follow-  up academic year activities; 8) Expose students to research methods 
              using resources provided by The University of Toledo's Carlson  Library,
              faculty, students, and campus, as well as community role  models and parents. 
              Members of civic and community organizations  will also serve as role models,
              mentors and guest lecturers.    Scientific Disciplines:  Science, Mathematics,
              Engineering and  Computer Science.  Programs to Which Proposal is Submitted: 
              Summer Science Camp.  Instructional Grade Levels: Grades 7, 8, and 9.
