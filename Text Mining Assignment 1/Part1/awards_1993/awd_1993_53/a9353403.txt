Abstract    :
              9353403  Crosby    This project, sponsored by the Division of Chemical
              Education,  Inc., will provide workshops for 100 teachers in the Lewisburg, PA 
              area in conjunction with the 13th Biennial Conference on Chemical  Education. 
              The teachers will be provided special workshops  concerning the latest
              techniques in laboratory and demonstration  activities and will then
              participate in follow-up workshops to  assure that new materials and techniques
              are implemented in the  classroom.
