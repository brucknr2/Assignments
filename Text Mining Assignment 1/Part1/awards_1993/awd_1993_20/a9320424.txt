              9320424  Robbins       This award if for the Department of Energy (DOE) budget 
              request for FY 1994 includes funding for an asymmetric B-  factory at a site to
              be determined and specifies that the DOE  and the National Science Foundation
              conduct a technical review  of the B-factory proposals from Cornell University
              and from  the Stanford Linear Accelerator Center.  Mr. Leon Panetta,  Director
              of the Office of Management and Budget, directed the  two agencies to carry out
              the review.  This award covers one-  half of the joint review committee
              expenses, as agreed by the  two agencies.  ***
