Abstract    :
              9370906  Magee    This award funds the last year of a three year continuing
              award  under the "Self-sufficient Partnership for Research" Program for  New
              Jersey Institute of Technology's Industry/University  Cooperative Research
              Center for Hazardous Substance Management.   The Center continue to meet the
              renewal criteria of the  Industry/University Cooperative Research Centers
              Program.    Additional funding has been provided for a non-voting membership in
               the Center with funds provided by the U.S. Department of Energy's 
              Environmental Restoration and Waste Management Office.    Funding is also
              provided for equipment for a disabled graduate  student of the Center to enable
              him to study "An Advanced Computer  Based Model of Catalytic Incinerators and
              Combustors".    The Program Manager recommends the New Jersey Institute of 
              Technology be awarded:  $30,000 for the last year of a three-year  continuing
              award;  $10,000 for one year with funds provided by the  U.S. Department of
              Energy, and $8,199 for one year with funds  provided by the Education and Human
              Resources Division.
