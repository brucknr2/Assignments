Abstract    :
              Topic areas covered by the                                                     
              conference are:  Active                                                        
              Semiconductor Devices, which                                                   
              includes aspects of active                                                     
              semiconductor-based photonic                                                   
              components fabricated in integrated                                            
              from, or which are suitable for                                                
              integration.  Nonlinear pertain to                                             
              nonlinear devices in planar and                                                
              channel waveguides.  These include                                             
              experiments on and theory of                                                   
              frequency conversion devices such                                              
              as second harmonic generators and                                              
              parametric oscillators; devices                                                
              based on third order phenomena such                                            
              as all-optical switching, nonlinear                                            
              couplers, and spatial solutions,                                               
              and photorefractive phenomena.                                                 
              Modeling, Numerical Simulation                                                 
              describe techniques and                                                        
              mathematical algorithms for                                                    
              modeling and numerical simulation,                                             
              or theory, of photonic devices and                                             
              materials, and results obtained by                                             
              using such tools.  Waveguides and                                              
              Waveguide Devices include passive                                              
              and active dielectric waveguide and                                            
              waveguide devices, circuit                                                     
              integration and coupling, and their                                            
              applications.
