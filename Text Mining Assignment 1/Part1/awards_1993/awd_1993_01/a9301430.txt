Abstract    :
                The inherent complexity of computational and data manipulation  problems is
              investigated.  Specific topics studied  include Boolean  circuit and
              communication complexity, decision  tree models, and the complexity  of data
              structures.  A  concentrated effort is made at resolving some of  the long 
              standing open problems.  Techniques from  combinatorics, probability theory,
              and topology provides the  main  mathematical tools for the project.
