Abstract    :
              9352992  Acevedo    The University of the Sacred Heart will offer a five  week
              summer residential project for 15 students entering grades 9  through 12.  The
              project focus is interdisciplinary and will focus  on  environmental issues. 
              Participants are exposed to a broad  range of scientific issues through
              conferences, lectures and close  and personal contact with practicing
              scientists in different  fields. Students work on and complete research
              projects in the  summer and academic year follow-up portion of the project. 
              Ethics  and philosophy of science activities along with career explorations 
              complete the program.  ***
