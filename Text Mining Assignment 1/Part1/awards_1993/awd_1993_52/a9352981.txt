Abstract    :
              9352981  Farley    Virginia Commonwealth University seeks to initiate a 3-week
              summer  commuter, EAI Young Scholars program for 30 students. The Program 
              provides interesting and challenging activities in mathematics and  physics for
              African American students entering grade 7. During the  summer students will
              engage in hands-on laboratory activities and  discussion on the physics of
              robots, and introduced to research  methodology activities by university
              scientists and mathematicians.  Academic year follow-up activities include
              regular mentor contact  and group activities through the Richmond Area
              Mathematics and  Science Center.
