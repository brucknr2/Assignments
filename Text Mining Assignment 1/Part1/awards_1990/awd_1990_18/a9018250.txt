Abstract    :
                   This award will partially support the Atlanta Conference                  
              on the SSC:  Industrial and Scientific Opportunities; Atlanta,                 
              Georgia; November 14-15, 1990.  The conference will focus on                   
              the scientific and industrial opportunity at the SSC.  Invited                 
              papers will be given on the SSC project and its relation to the                
              scientific and industrial effort in the U.S. as well as on the                 
              physics at SSC and on detector research and development for the                
              SSC experiments.
