Abstract    :
                   This Special Minority Award provides a second year of                     
              support for Jon Velazquez, a graduate student at Cornell                       
              University.  The award will allow Mr. Velazquez to focus his                   
              full attention on research in the coming year.  This award is                  
              an integral part of the strategy of the National Science                       
              Foundation to strengthen and increase minority participation in                
              the field of physics.
