Abstract    :
                                                                                             
              This award is for renewed support for the                                      
              Natural Hazards Research and Applications                                      
              Information Center at the University of                                        
              Colorado in Boulder.  The Center monitors and                                  
              disseminates hazards research information to                                   
              multiple users--administrators,                                                
              practitioners, policy makers, and scientists.                                  
              The Center publishes and distributes the                                       
              Natural Hazards Observer to over 9,000                                         
              subscribers six times a year, and publishes                                    
              monographs, working papers, bibliographies                                     
              and special publications.  It holds an annual                                  
              workshop involving users and producers of                                      
              hazards research, and it provides information                                  
              on a day-to-day basis in response to                                           
              inquiries.  The Center conducts modest                                         
              research activities relating to societal                                       
              response to natural hazards and it supports                                    
              researchers throughout the nation as they                                      
              carry out "quick response" studies of                                          
              specific hazard events.
