Abstract    :
              This is a block travel grant to partially                                      
              support persons attending the Fourth                                           
              International Symposium on Harmonics in Power                                  
              Systems in October, 1990 in Budapest, Hungary.                                 
              The meeting is an IEEE co-sponsored meeting                                    
              which has been held in the past in the United                                  
              States and Canada.  This is an international                                   
              meeting which typically attracts many                                          
              Europeans.  This grant will enhance the                                        
              representation of U.S. power engineers at this                                 
              meeting.
