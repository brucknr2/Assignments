Abstract    :
              The grant is in support of a conference on "Optical Amplifiers" to be          
              held August 6-8, 1990 in Monterey, CA.  Topics include:                        
                                                                                             
              - Fiber Amplifiers:                                                            
                   - Rare earth doped fiber                                                  
                   - Brillouin fiber                                                         
                   - Power considerations                                                    
                   - Noise characteristic                                                    
                   - Performance characteristics, etc.                                       
                                                                                             
              - Semiconductor Amplifiers:                                                    
                   - Semiconductor lasers                                                    
                   - Power considerations                                                    
                   - Noise characteristic                                                    
                   - Performance characteristics, etc.                                       
                                                                                             
              - Systems Requirements and Applications:                                       
                   - Long haul                                                               
                   - Ultra high speed                                                        
                   - Subscriber, CATV                                                        
                   - Switching/signal process                                                
                   - Sensors                                                                 
                                                                                             
              - Components:                                                                  
                   - Pump Lasers                                                             
                   - Isolators                                                               
                   - Couplers                                                                
                   - Packaging                                                               
                   - Stabilization                                                           
                                                                                             
              Funds will be used to support travel for students and invited                  
              speakers.
