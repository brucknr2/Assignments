Abstract    :
                                                                                             
              Chestnut Hill College in partnership with Rohm and Haas has                    
              received NSF support to improve hands-on science application                   
              activities in Delaware Valley schools and to foster direct                     
              involvement of scientists in the improvement of science                        
              education.  The activities include (a) summer six-day                          
              internships for teachers at Rohm and Haas, (b) follow-up to                    
              that experience to generate lessons for students, (c)                          
              development of strategies to insure use of materials and                       
              extension of the collaborative efforts.  Fifty-four teachers                   
              will be affected, 18 each summer for three summers, by the                     
              project.
