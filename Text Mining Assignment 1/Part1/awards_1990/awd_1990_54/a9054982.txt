Abstract    :
                                                                                             
              The New Jersey Institute of Technology will initiate a three-                  
              week, commuter Young Scholars project in Environmental Waste                   
              Management for 30 students (15 entering grade 8; 15 entering                   
              grade 9).                                                                      
                                                                                             
              These students will be introduced to the current problems,                     
              available solutions and research involving the critical issues                 
              associated with substances management.  The participating                      
              students will work with faculty of the NJIT Hazardous Substance                
              Management Center on research projects related to this theme.                  
              Students will be instructed in the scientific method and the                   
              engineering science associated with hazardous substance                        
              management and motivated to pursue careers in science and                      
              engineering.  The program will include classroom and laboratory                
              activities, seminars and field trips.
