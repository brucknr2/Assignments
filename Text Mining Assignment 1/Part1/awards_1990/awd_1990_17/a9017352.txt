Abstract    :
                                                                                             
              This is an interagency agreement between NSF and the                           
              Department of State (DOS) to reimburse DOS for the cost                        
              of administrative services provided to NSF staff                               
              stationed in Tokyo, Japan, and Paris, France.  In                              
              addition, support is given to NSF travelers to Tokyo,                          
              Paris, Bangkok, and Tel Aviv.  These costs are a                               
              necessary part of NSF's overseas activities.
