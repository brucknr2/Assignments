Abstract    :
                   The work to be carried out comprises the following topics:                 
                  1.  The experimental Study of metastable helium atoms and                 
              molecules at extremely low temperatures.                                       
                   2.  Interactions of helium metastables with liquid helium                 
              surfaces.                                                                      
                   3.  Magneto-optical trapping of atomic helium metastables.                
                   4.  Penning ionization dynamics.                                          
                   5.  Further development of polarized electron sources.
