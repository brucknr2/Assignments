Abstract    :
              This award supports U.S. participation in the Sixth                            
              Antarctic Earth Sciences Symposium to be held September                        
              9-13, 1991, at Saitama, Japan.  The symposium has four                         
              principal themes:                                                              
                                                                                             
                   1) Tectonic Evolution of the Antarctic Crust in                           
                   Gondwana                                                                  
                                                                                             
                   2) West Antarctica and East Antarctic/West Antarctica                     
                   Relationship                                                              
                                                                                             
                   3) Crustal and Upper Mantle Structure of the Antarctic                    
                   Continent and Surrounding Oceans                                          
                                                                                             
                   4) Cenozoic Glacial History of Antarctica and Paleo-                      
                   environments of the Southern Oceans                                       
                                                                                             
              The U.S. research effort in earth sciences encompasses many                    
              of the stated themes and sub-themes of the meeting, and a                      
              substantial number of U.S. earth scientists can be expected                    
              to attend and present results.
