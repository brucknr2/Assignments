              device characteristics of layered heterostructure devices (GaAs, GaAlAs) for 
              resonance tunneling diodes, quantum dots, etc.  The scattering algorithms will
              be adapted from computational chemistry ton nano-device applications including
              the effects of external magnetic fields.    The PI will collaborate with the
              National Center for Computational Electronics at University of Illinois to
              obtain the materials parameters as well as the important physics to be resolve
