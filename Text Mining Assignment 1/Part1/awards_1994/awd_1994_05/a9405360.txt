                  9405360  Foley      This is a group travel award for American computer
              engineers and  scientists to attend a workshop on the foundations and future 
              developments in the area of human-computer interaction in  multimedia systems
              in Dagstuhl, Germany.  Topics covered in the  workshop will include, but not be
              limited to, aspects of user  interfaces involving the purposes of multimedia
              systems, media  encoding and compression, multimedia support systems and 
              abstractions, storage, databases, communications and networking for 
              multimedia, electronic document handling and mailing, conferencing  and
              collaborative computing, and virtual reality and multimodal  interfaces.  A
              record of the proceedings of the workshop is to be  produced and published.
