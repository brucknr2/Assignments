              9402046  Gee       The National Science Foundation requires prompt, fair, 
              impartial, and complete investigations of complaints of  discrimination filed
              by NSF employees or applicants for employment  with NSF that are based on race,
              color, religion, national origin,  sex, age, physical or mental disability, or
              reprisal for having  filed a prior complaint of discrimination, or for opposing
              any  practice made unlawful by Title VII of the Civil Rights Act of  1964, as
              amended; the Rehabilitation Act of 1973, as amended; the  Age Discrimination in
              Employment Act of 1967, as amended; or the  Equal Pay Act.
