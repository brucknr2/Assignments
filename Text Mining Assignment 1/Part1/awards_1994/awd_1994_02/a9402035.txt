Abstract    :
              ABSTRACT      9402035 BARTKE   The 1994 Winter Gordon Conference on Prolactin
              will provide a forum for presentation and thorough discussion of the most
              recent developments in the study of prolactin (PRL) and other hormones of the
              PRL growth hormone family.  The topics to be covered include: regulation of PRL
              gene expression, PRL and GH receptors, local synthesis and actions of PRL in
              the brain, novel effects of PRL and mechanisms of PRL action, placental
              lactogens, conditions of PRL excess and PRL deficiency as well as novel
              findings on regulation of PRL release.  Data obtained in different species
              ranging from non mammalian vertebrates to the human will be presented.  All
              participants will have an opportunity to present their recent findings in
              poster sessions.   ***
