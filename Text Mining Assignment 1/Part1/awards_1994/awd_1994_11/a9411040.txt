Abstract    :
              This award provides travel support for U.S. graduate students to attend the
              Chapman Conference on  Water Vapor in the Climate System, sponsored by the
              American Geophysical Union.  The conference  will be held in Jekyll Island,
              Georgia in the fall of 1994.  The conference focus is on the understanding  the
              role of water vapor in the climate system and how to best to observe and
              measure water vapor  for climate purposes.
