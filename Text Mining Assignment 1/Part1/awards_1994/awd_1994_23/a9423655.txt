Abstract    :
              9423655  Schorr    This award covers international travel for the User Services
              Area  Director of the Internet Engineering Task Force (IETF).  The User 
              Services Area provides an international forum for people interested  in all
              levels of user services to identify and initiate projects  designed to improve
              the quality of information available to users  of the Internet.  The Area
              Director plans trips to London for RARE  meetings to Amsterdam for RIPE
              meetings, and to Singapore for the  INET '95 conference.  Trip reports are
              published in the Internet  Monthly Report.
