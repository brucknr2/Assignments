              9423071  Walker  This award will offset travel expenses of U.S.-based students
              attending the Third Hutton Symposium on the Origin of Granites and Related
              Rocks.  The conference is designed for scientists with an interest in granites
              and related rocks, and will be held at the University of Maryland at College
              Park.  Participants will be involved in oral sessions, poster sessions, and
              field trips.
