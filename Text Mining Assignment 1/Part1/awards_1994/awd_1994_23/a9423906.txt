Abstract    :
              Dr. Albert J. Fry and other members of the Chemistry Department of   Wesleyan
              University are being supported to establish a Research   Experiences for
              Undergraduates (REU) site in chemistry.  For the period   1995-97, six
              undergraduate minority students will spend ten weeks each   summer actively
              engaged in a variety of research projects drawn from the   traditional areas of
              chemistry.  A unique feature of this program is that   students will be
              recruited primarily from eleven historically black   colleges and universities
              with which Wesleyan has established working   relationships.
