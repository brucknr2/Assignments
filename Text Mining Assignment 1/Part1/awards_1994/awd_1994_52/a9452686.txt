              9452686  Orzech    Oregon State University seeks to initiate a three-week, 
              residential, Young Scholars Project in physics and mathematics for  36 students
              entering grades 8 and 9. The participants will be  members of the existing
              Oregon State University  Science and  Mathematics Investigative Learning
              Experiences (SMILE) Program that  offers academic year
              mathematics/science/computer enrichment  activities for minority students,
              mostly Native American and  Hispanic, in rural Oregon communities.
