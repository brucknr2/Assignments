Abstract    :
              9452745  Pantano    Santa Fe Community College seeks to initiate a four-week,
              commuter,  Young Scholars Project for 24 students entering grades 8 and 9. The 
              project is an interdisciplinary program in life science. Students  will
              participate in activities involving scientific exploration,  laboratory
              techniques, data gathering, and data analysis in two  main areas: Water Quality
              and Artificial Life. Field trips and  follow-up research-related activities are
              an integral part of the  program.
