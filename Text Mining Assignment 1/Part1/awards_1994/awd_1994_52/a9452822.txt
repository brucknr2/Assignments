Abstract    :
              9452822  Marable              Tennessee Technology University will initiate a
              four-week,  residential, Young Scholars project in engineering for 50 students 
              entering grades 11 and 12. The students are primarily from the  southern
              Appalachian region of the United States. The program will  consist of
              individual research projects, field trips to various  sites of engineering
              significance, and lectures and discussions in  an Introduction to Engineering
              series, Introduction to Engineering  Design, and Energy Utilization.
