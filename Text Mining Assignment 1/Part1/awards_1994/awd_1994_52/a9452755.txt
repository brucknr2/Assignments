Abstract    :
              9452755  Sally                          The University of Chicago will initiate
              a four week, commuter,  Young Scholars program in the fields of mathematics,
              physics, and  computer science which will offer science enrichment activities
              for  108 students entering grades 7-12. The program will emphasize  research
              participation in mathematics. The mathematical focus will  be on number theory,
              with the content and sophistication varying  according to the grade level of
              the participants. Students will be  provided with a rich and diverse experience
              in mathematics, physics  and computer science.
