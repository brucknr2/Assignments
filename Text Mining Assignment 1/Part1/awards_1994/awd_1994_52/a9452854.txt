Abstract    :
              9452854  Powe  This proposal requests funding for the 1994 National EPSCoR 
              Conference.  The conference will be held in Jackson, Mississippi,  on December
              4-7, 1994.  The conference will feature a poster  session by the EPSCoR states.
               The program will include  panelists, presentations, key speakers, and
              discussion sessions  focusing on the theme, "EPSCoR:  Policies to Build Science
              and  Technology."  Travel grants will be provided for up to five  people from
              each EPSCoR state (exclusive of the Project Director  and Project
              Administrator) who are influential in the development  of science and
              technology infrastructure and programs.  These may  represent state government,
              academia, or the private sector.  A  conference proceedings will be prepared
              and distributed.
