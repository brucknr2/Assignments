              9420654  Mitchell    This award adds three higher education institutions to the
              MOREnet  state network:              Moberly Area Community College           
              Three Rivers Community College            University of Health Sciences   
              Faculty and students at the institutions benefit from access to the  resources
              of the Internet, including libraries and supercomputers.   In addition, they
              are able to communicate and collaborate with  colleagues at other campuses in
              pursuit of research and educational  projects.  The award provides partial
              support of the project for  two years.
