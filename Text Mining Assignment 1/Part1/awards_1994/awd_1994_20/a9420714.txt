Abstract    :
                This award will support travel of  U.S. participants in a U.S.-CECAM workshop
              on Hybrid Quantum and Classical Mechanical Methods for Simulation of
              Biopolymers, Lyons, France,  May 9-11, 1995.  The co-organizers of the workshop
              are Jiali Gao of the State University of New York at Buffalo and Martin Field
              of the Institute for Structural Biology in Grenoble, France. The workshop is
              organized under the auspices of CECAM (Centre Europeen de Calcul Atomique et
              Moleculaire), a Western European regional organization supported by the
              national research institutions of France, Italy, Netherlands, Belgium, United
              Kingdom and Switzerland.   The objective of the workshop is to discuss and
              explore future research on computer simulations of biopolymers in order to
              determine their structure and function. Application and development of hybrid
              quantum and molecular mechanical approaches to the study of complex biological
              systems will be discussed.
