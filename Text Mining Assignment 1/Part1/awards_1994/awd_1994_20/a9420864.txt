Abstract    :
                This award supplies funds to the University of Pittsburgh to increase the
              training of their Ph.D. graduate students in Cultural Anthropology.  The funds
              will be used to help the students go to the places they anticipate doing their
              dissertation field work, or else to accompany a faculty adviser to a field site
              to work with the adviser in a traineeship.  This award will increase the
              scientific capacity of US social scientists by expanding opportunities for
              training in ethnographic techniques of field research before the critically
              important Ph.D. research.
