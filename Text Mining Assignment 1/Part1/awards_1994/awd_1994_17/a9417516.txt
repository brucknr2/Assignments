Abstract    :
              9417516  Smith  This project connects Southern California college to the
              Internet  through the California State University network (CSUnet) over a 56 
              kbps line.  Faculty and students benefit from access to the  resources of the
              Internet, including libraries and supercomputers.   In addition, they are able
              to communicate and collaborate with  colleagues at other institutions in
              pursuit of research and  educational projects.  The award provides partial
              support of the  project for two years.
