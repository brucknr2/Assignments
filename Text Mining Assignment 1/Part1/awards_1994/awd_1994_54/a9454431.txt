Abstract    :
              9454431  Deutschman                  The New Jersey Institute of Technology
              (NJIT) will initiate a three-week, commuter, Young Scholars Project in
              Environmental Waste Management for 30 students entering grades 8 and 9. Under
              the mentoring of research faculty members of NJIT, the high potential, high
              ability students (predominately minority) will be instructed in the scientific
              method and engage in research in the engineering science associated with
              hazardous substance management and motivated to pursue careers in science and
              engineering. Additional activities include classroom and laboratory activities,
              seminars, and field trips.
