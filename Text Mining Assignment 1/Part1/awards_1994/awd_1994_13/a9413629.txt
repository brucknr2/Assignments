Abstract    :
              This Small Business Technology  Transfer (STTR) Phase I research project will
              develop a parts-per-billion (ppb) moisture concentration sensing instrument
              based on a ring down cavity (RDC) spectroscopy technique.  Commercial
              application includes characterizing the purity of a variety of gases used in
              the manufacture of semiconductors.
