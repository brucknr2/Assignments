              9413091  DuVall       Building on their existing successful partnership, East 
              Carolina University and Black and Decker will use industrial sites  and
              research in lieu of traditional classrooms and graduate theses  for a masters
              degree.  One hundred twenty people from defense  industry and military sites in
              Maryland and North Carolina will be  able to earn a Master of Science in
              Industrial Technology through  research projects and interactive compressed
              video courses at six  Black and Decker sites through research projects and
              interactive  video courses.
