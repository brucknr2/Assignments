Abstract    :
               This grant supported a workshop on Numerical Solution   of Markov Chains.  The 
              area  is of current high research activity and wide   applicability.  The
              workshop  brought together three  groups of researchers working in this  
              field:  applied probabilists, numerical analysts, and system    engineers.  The
              goals of  the workshop included:    - To foster co-operation among diverse
              researchers, to   minimize  unnecessary duplication and to identify new long- 
              term research objectives.   - To bring up-to-date and to inform researchers who
              work in   branch of this area, the work being undertaken in  different   areas.
                - To produce a comprehensive set of proceedings that will   become a
              significant reference work.   - To launch a repository of numerical examples
              that arise in  Markov chain  applications, and to couple it with sparse  
              matrix manipulation  procedures and well-tested and robust  numerical solution 
               methods.
