Abstract    :
              Symbiotech, Incorporated  proposes to determine the feasibility of using
              bacteriorhodopsin to construct an optical detector and discriminating device.
              The proposed device will provide a new means for gathering and deciphering
              optical information. It is anticipated the proposed device coupled to
              appropriate algorithms will possess the capacity for size and shape
              discrimination, motion detection, and coordinates determination.
