Abstract    :
              The PIs propose to investigate the causes of highway accidents.  The PIs will
              develop fault trees based on  the databases of state and national
              organizations.  From these data the PIs will determine causal  relationships
              among the vehicle features, driver behavior, and accident occurrence.  Finally,
               guidelines will be generated for vehicle design modifications based upon the
              driver errors identified  as most frequently as being a cause of accidents. 
              The PIs will join with the General Motors Research  Laboratories on this
              Private Sector Research Initiative.
