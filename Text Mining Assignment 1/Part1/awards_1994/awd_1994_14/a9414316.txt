Abstract    :
              9414316  Sieben    This project connects Texas Lutheran College to NSFNET
              through the  THEnet state network.  Faculty and students at the school benefit 
              from access to the resources of the Internet, including libraries  and
              supercomputers.  In addition, they are able to communicate and  collaborate
              with colleagues at other institutions in pursuit of  research and educational
              projects.  The award provides partial  support of the project for two years.
