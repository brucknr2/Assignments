              9401736  York   This award provides funds to bring together a group of computer
              scientists, mathematicians, educators, and social scientists for a series of 2
              workshops to:      1.  Identify the root causes of the lack of        
              participation of minorities in Computer         Science research and education 
                  2.  To develop specific mechanisms which federal        agencies can
              implement to begin solving the        problem    The workshop will address four
              broad areas:      1.  Computer Science pipeline issues      2.  Mentoring of
              minority graduate students and        junior faculty      3.  Academic
              professional development      4.  Race and culture issues.  ***
