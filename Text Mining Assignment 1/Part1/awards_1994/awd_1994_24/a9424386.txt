              9424386  Klafter  This award supports the travel of U.S. delegates for their 
              participation in the IEEE International Conference of  Robotics and /automation
              to be held I Nagoya, japan from May  21 to 27, 1995. The Conference will bring
              together U.S. and  foreign researchers for information exchange, and for 
              identification of  challenging research issues related to  the areas of
              robotics, automation, computer-aided-design,  computer-integrated-manufacturing
              and advanced computer  technology required in flexible manufacturing. A
              selection  committee is responsible for the choice of participants to  receive
              funds according to a pre-established et of  criteria.***
