Abstract    :
              The objective of this project is to develop and validate 3D vortex element
              methods on  both fine and coarse grained parallel architectures.   The
              numerical schemes are based on  discretization of the vorticity field into a
              large number of Lagrangian elements, and transport  of  these elements along
              particle trajectories.  This award will, in particular, be used to support
              Prof.  S. Huberson's participation in constructing and optimizing the
              algorithms.
