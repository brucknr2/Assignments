Abstract    :
              This Small Business Innovation Research Phase I project will examine the
              feasibility of developing a mathematical software environment for classroom use
              in high schools. The design philosophy will be driven by the National Council
              of Teachers of Mathematics' (NCTM) Curriculum and Evaluation Standards for
              School Mathematics and the software system will run on a Personal Digital
              Assistant (PDA.) Major components of the system will include: a geometry
              sketching facility, algebraic manipulation, numerical calculation,
              trigonometric evaluations and graphing. All these components will interact with
              each other and will be accessed via a user interface suitable for use by
              high-school students. The project will evaluate these components with the aid
              of teachers and high school students and, subsequently, produce a software
              architectural design suitable for implementation in Phases II and III.
