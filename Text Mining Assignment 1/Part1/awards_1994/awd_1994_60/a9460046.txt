Abstract    :
              This Small Business Innovation Research Phase I project addresses the
              long-standing UV sensitivity problem in CCDs. UV quantum efficiency (QE) could
              be improved by attaching a `UV screen` to the face of the CCD that would also
              provide visible rejection. Research would focus on preparation and evaluation
              of the screen, which would be fabricated from a lumogen-coated dichroic mirror.
              A back-illuminated CCD with the UV screen attached could achieve nearly 50% QE
              from 200 to 400 nm.
