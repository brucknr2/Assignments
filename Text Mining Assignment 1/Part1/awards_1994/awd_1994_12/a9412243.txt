Abstract    :
              Microporous solids will be made by polymerizing one phase of bicontinuous 
              microemulsions.  Preliminary experiments show these solids can have surface
              areas over 1000 m2/g and  can selectively adsorb heavy metals like copper. 
              These high surface areas are to be obtained  routinely with more crosslinking,
              polymerizable detergents, and altered detergent concentrations.
