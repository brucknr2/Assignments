Abstract    :
              A series of three Calculus Reform Workshops are being held during              
              the summer of 1992.  Each five day workshop has 26 participants and            
              includes the following:                                                        
                                                                                             
              - overview of calculus reform projects                                         
              - extensive participant work with specific reformed approach                   
              - experienced curriculum reformers as workshop instructors                     
              - participant development of curriculum materials                              
              - establishment of support network among participants.                         
                                                                                             
              The Iowa, Southern California, and New Jersey Sections of the MAA              
              are each hosting a workshop.
