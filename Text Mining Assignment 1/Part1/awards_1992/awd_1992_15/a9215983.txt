Abstract    :
              This award provides infrastructure support for the development of              
              the research and educational activities of the Hampton University              
              Experimental Laboratory for promoting Education and Research.  The             
              support includes computing resources, as well as awards to                     
              undergraduate and graduate students.  The investigators will be                
              conducting research in the areas of (1) high-speed/parallel                    
              computing, (2) software engineering, (3) computer graphics, and (4)            
              artificial intelligence/expert system development.
