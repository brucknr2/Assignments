Abstract    :
                                                                                              
                  This project will study the middle atmosphere and lower                   
              thermosphere (60-150 km altitude) through the continued analysis               
              and interpretation of AIDA (Arecibo Initiative in Dynamics of the              
              Atmosphere) '89 data.  Moreover, a new observing program initiated             
              at Arecibo will examine neutral wave motion in the lower                       
              thermosphere, extending past AIDA studies of atmospheric dynamics              
              into a higher altitude domain.
