Abstract    :
                   This award to Editorial Experts Inc., will assist the                     
              Directorate for Engineering (ENG) with editorial and conference                
              support.  The goal is to make the results of basic engineering                 
              research available to other researchers, industry, and the                     
              public.  The quick and accurate dissemination of such information              
              from ENG through the efforts of Editorial Experts, Inc. will                   
              undoubtedly have a major impact in the advancement of the                      
              Nation's technological progress.
