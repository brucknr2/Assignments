Abstract    :
                   This award provides partial support for a full-time research              
              technician who will operate the electron microprobe system in the              
              Department of Geological Sciences at the University of south                   
              Carolina. The University is committed to providing the remaining               
              funds required.                                                                
                   The electron microprobe instrument provides quantitative                  
              chemical analysis of small inhomogeneous rock and mineral samples              
              needed for the research and teaching programs of members of the                
              Department of Geological Sciences.
