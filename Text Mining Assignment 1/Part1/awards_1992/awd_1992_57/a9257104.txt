Abstract    :
                  This project addresses  research in storage management for high performance
              computer  architectures.  There is need for technology for comparing storage 
              managers relative to a given workload characterization to determine  which will
              perform best.  Research techniques to accomplish this are  based on a
              combination of analysis, simulation, and prototype  implementations. 
              Applications such as audio and video are imposing  new requirements for
              performance and guaranteed response times.    The introduction of object
              oriented systems introduces new and  different request patterns.  This
              illustrates the direction of new hardware  technology and new application
              demands that will provide a focus for  the research.
