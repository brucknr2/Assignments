Abstract    :
                                                                                             
              Wiley College seeks to provide a six week summer residential                   
              Young Scholars program in interdisciplinary science and                        
              mathematics for twenty students in the eleventh and twelfth                    
              grades. The participants residing within the East Texas                        
              region will be provided with a research experience and                         
              experiences in hands-on laboratory activities in chemistry,                    
              physics, mathematics and computer science.
