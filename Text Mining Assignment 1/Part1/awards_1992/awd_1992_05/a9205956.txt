Abstract    :
              The principal investigator will study  fluid-rock interaction under            
              high-pressure conditions. To do so he will experimentally investig-            
              ate the solubilities of talc+enstatite, enstatite+forsterite,                  
              albite, and jadeite at high pressure. This will provide constraints            
              necessary to evaluate mass transfer processes in subduction zones.
