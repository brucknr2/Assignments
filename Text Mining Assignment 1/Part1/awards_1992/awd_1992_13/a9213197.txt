Abstract    :
              The Gel Permeation Chromatography                                              
              (GPC) system will be used for                                                  
              research in tissue engineering, and                                            
              drug release from bioerodible                                                  
              copolymer matrices.  The research                                              
              work in tissue engineering involves                                            
              the development of novel                                                       
              biomaterials for cell                                                          
              transplantation to regenerate                                                  
              metabolic organs and structural                                                
              tissues.  Biodegradable polymers                                               
              with high porosity and large                                                   
              surface/volume ratios can provide a                                            
              sturdy, yet temporary, scaffold for                                            
              osteoblast transplantation to                                                  
              regenerate bone.  For drug release                                             
              studies, the GPC will supplement in                                            
              situ video microscopy, image                                                   
              analysis, and computer simulations                                             
              already used in the laboratory for                                             
              the design of polymer devices with                                             
              optimal release characteristics.
