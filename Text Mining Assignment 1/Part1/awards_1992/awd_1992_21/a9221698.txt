              This is a travel award for exploratory visits to Stanford                      
              University and the Universidade Gama Filho in Rio de Janeiro,                  
              Brazil to meet with prospective mentors, establish contacts with               
              other scholars involved in racial studies, and refine research                 
              plans for postdoctoral study.  Initial contacts have been made with            
              Dr. Claude Steele, Stanford U., and Dr. Aroldo Rodrigues,                      
              Universidade Gama Filho, who have expressed interest in serving as             
              postdoctoral mentor.
