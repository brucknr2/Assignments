Abstract    :
              The Department of Geology is acquiring the components to create                
              a computer network, equipped with essential hardware and                       
              software, for the use of undergraduate students.  The                          
              laboratory is being used by students at all levels -                           
              introductory through those engaged in research projects.  In                   
              addition, to the use of commercial software, the faculty is                    
              creating a substantial number of new programs that is being                    
              made available to other educational institutions.
