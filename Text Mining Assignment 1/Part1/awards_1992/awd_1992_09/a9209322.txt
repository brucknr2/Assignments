Abstract    :
              This project will provide both new                                             
              theoretical means for predicting                                               
              rates of hindered transport and new                                            
              experimental methods for measuring                                             
              transport rates.  Progress in                                                  
              modeling hindered transport will be                                            
              made by extending the effective                                                
              medium theory of the Principal                                                 
              Investigator to include the effects                                            
              of local hydrodynamic and                                                      
              electostatic interactions.  In                                                 
              addition, a method for dynamically                                             
              simulating the motion of a                                                     
              spherical macromolecule in a bed of                                            
              fibers will be developed and                                                   
              implemented.  The simulations will                                             
              provide a versatile approach to                                                
              studying a variety of fiber                                                    
              configurations and solute fiber                                                
              interactions, and will provide                                                 
              results that can be compared                                                   
              directly with the analytical                                                   
              results of the effective medium                                                
              theory.
