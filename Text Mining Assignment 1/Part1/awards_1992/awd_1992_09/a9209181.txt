              9209181  Anthes            This agreement between the Foundation and the
              University  Corporation for Atmospheric Research (UCAR) defines the manner in 
              which the National Center for Atmospheric Research (NCAR) shall  continue to
              support research in atmospheric sciences and related  sciences.  The primary
              purpose of this agreement with UCAR is the  support of NCAR as defined in
              Scientific Program Order #1.   Additional scientific program orders define
              other programs  continued by UCAR under the provisions of this agreement.  ***
