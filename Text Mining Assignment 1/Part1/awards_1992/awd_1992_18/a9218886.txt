Abstract    :
              This grant is for the partial                                                  
              support of a Symposium on                                                      
              Computational Neuroscience at                                                  
              Indiana University-Purdue                                                      
              University at Indianapolis  on                                                 
              October 17, 1992.  This symposium                                              
              will be a Satellite Meeting of the                                             
              1992 IEEE Systems, Man, Cybernetics Conference which will                      
              be held in Chicago on October 18th                                             
              to 21st.  The symposium will                                                   
              emphasize an area which was the                                                
              subject of a special initiative;                                               
              i.e.,  Biosystems Analysis and                                                 
              Control, which involved two                                                    
              directorates and three divisions.                                              
              The symposium also will act as a                                               
              follow up to the NSF sponsored                                                 
              workshop on "Bio-Control by Neural                                             
              Networks" which was held in May,                                               
              1990.  It will promote the                                                     
              interdisciplinary investigations                                               
              between engineers, mathematicians,                                             
              computer scientists and                                                        
              neuroscientists into the area of                                               
              computational neuroscience.
