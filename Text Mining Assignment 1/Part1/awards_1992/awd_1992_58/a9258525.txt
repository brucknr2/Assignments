Abstract    :
                 Current research on a first-order functional language (NESL) that  supplies
              parallelism through a set of data-parallel constructs based  on vectors is
              extended in several directions.  One is the  testing of the language via
              scientific applications algorithms.  Another is the addition of polymorphic
              types to the language.  Yet  another is the examination of lazy load-balancing
              based upon dynamic  testing.  In addition, implementations on several newer
              high  performance machines is carried out.
