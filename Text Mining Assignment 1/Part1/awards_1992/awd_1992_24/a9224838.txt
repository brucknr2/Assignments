Abstract    :
              The study will document the                                                    
              response to Hurricane Andrew                                                   
              warning process; ascertain                                                     
              residents' beliefs about the safety                                            
              of their residences, the need for                                              
              code improvement and better code                                               
              enforcement, and regulation or                                                 
              prohibition of mobile homes;                                                   
              evaluate the impact of concerns                                                
              about safety upon people's                                                     
              evacuation behavior in future                                                  
              hurricanes; and assess the changes                                             
              in concerns and beliefs with the                                               
              passage of time.  The research will                                            
              include observed changes in the                                                
              process of reconstruction.  These                                              
              results will be beneficial to                                                  
              researchers and officials involved                                             
              in the recovery efforts.
