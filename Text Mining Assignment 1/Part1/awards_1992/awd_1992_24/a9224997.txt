Abstract    :
              The 16th IFIP W.G. 7.3 International Symposium on Computer                     
              Performance Modelling, Measurement and Evaluation will be held in              
              Rome, Italy, during September 29-October 1, l993. This symposium               
              is the premier technical meeting that examines the state-of-the-               
              art in computer performance evaluation.  Increasing the level of               
              participation of scientists and engineers from the United States               
              will enhance the level of exchange and cooperation with European,              
              Asian, African, and Australian researchers.  Airfare for six                   
              United States-based participants to attend the symposium is                    
              provided.
