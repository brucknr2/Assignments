Abstract    :
                                                                                             
              The study will determine rock magnetic properties and mineralogical            
              characteristics of Jurassic basalts from ODP Leg 129 material which            
              will lead to an understanding of magnetic properties of these                  
              basalts, the Jurassic quiet zone and the source of high remanence              
              of old basalts.
