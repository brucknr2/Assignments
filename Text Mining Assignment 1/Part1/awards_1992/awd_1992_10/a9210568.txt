Abstract    :
               This research deals with scheduling algorithms for  parallel execution of tasks
              in parallel computations.  The  project develops innovative scheduling schemes
              for  parallel loops in numeric computations and dynamically spawned  work in
              AND and OR parallel symbolic computations.  The work  focuses on the design and
              implementation of scheduling  algorithms for shared and distributed memory
              machines in the  framework of the Chare-Kernel parallel programming system. 
              The  schemes is evaluated with a comprehensive set of  benchmark programs for
              both numeric and AND/OR computations
