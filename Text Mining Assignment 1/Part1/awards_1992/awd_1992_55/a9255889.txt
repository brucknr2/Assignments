Abstract    :
                                                                                             
              The University of Colorado at Denver seeks to provide for a                    
              five week commuter Young Scholars Program in chemistry. The                    
              program will serve fifteen students entering ninth and tenth                   
              grades in the Denver metropolitan area. The students will                      
              work on environmentally oriented research projects with                        
              emphasis on chemistry in the University of Colorado research                   
              laboratories. Other activities include the participation in                    
              problem solving and discussion sessions, field trips to                        
              local industry and government laboratories, and interacting                    
              with practicing scientists.
