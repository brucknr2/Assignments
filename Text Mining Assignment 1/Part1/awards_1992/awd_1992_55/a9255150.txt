Abstract    :
                                                                                             
              The goals of the Research Career for Minority                                  
              Scholars (RCMS) program are to attract and                                     
              retain minority students who have the                                          
              potential to do above average work in the                                      
              fields of chemistry and physics, and to give                                   
              these students the motivation, training and                                    
              opportunity to continue their studies in                                       
              chemistry and physics at the advanced degree                                   
              level.  To meet these goals, a recruitment,                                    
              training, mentoring and monitoring program                                     
              will be established.  The Program Director                                     
              (PD) will have overall responsibility for                                      
              meeting the goals of the program with the                                      
              assistance of the participating faculty.
