Abstract    :
                                                                                             
              The University of Northern Iowa will initiate a three-week,                    
              commuter, Early Alert Initiative Young Scholars project in                     
              Mathematics and Physics for 24 students entering grades 7,8.                   
                                                                                             
              This project emphasizes research participation in the study of                 
              energy transer, light, sound, probability, and statistics.                     
              Students conduct investigations and design experiments.  There also            
              are opportunities for students to engage in career exploration                 
              activities through interactions with scientists.
