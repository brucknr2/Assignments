Abstract    :
              The goal of the Capital Area Theory Seminar is to stimulate research           
              activity in theoretical computer science.  The plan is to have a talk          
              every other week at the University of Maryland, College Park, from             
              fall of l991 to spring of 1994.  The requested funds, with matching            
              funds from University of Maryland Institute for Advanced Computer              
              Studies, will be used to invite ten outside speakers per year.
