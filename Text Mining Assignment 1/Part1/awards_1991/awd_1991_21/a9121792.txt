Abstract    :
                   This award pays the license fees for permission for NSF to                
              use the Science Citation Index bibliometric data base for the                  
              years 1989 and 1990, in accordance with the Licensing Agreement                
              between NSF and the Institute for Scientific Information.                      
                   Initial uses of the data base by NSF will be to develop a                 
              variety of bibliometric analyses for use in the National Science               
              Board's Congressionally mandated Science & Engineering Indicators              
              volumes for 1991 and 1993.  Other uses within the scope of the                 
              Licensing Agreement may also be undertaken.
