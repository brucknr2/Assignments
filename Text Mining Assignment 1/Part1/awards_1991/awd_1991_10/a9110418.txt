Abstract    :
              This award to the National Academy of Sciences is for                          
              support of the activities of the Geotechnical Board during                     
              the one year period August 1, 1991, to July 31, 1992.  The                     
              Geotechnical Board exists within the National Research                         
              Council and its Commission on Engineering and Technical                        
              Systems.  The toatal budget is $400,000 out of which NSF                       
              will provide $25,000.  Funds are requested from 12 agencies                    
              in amounts ranging from $10,000 to $50,000.
