              The contractor will replace version 3.0 of the Electronic                      
              Proposal Submission System with version 4.0.  The contractor,                  
              in close coordination with OIS staff will:                                     
                                                                                             
                   a.  Provide programming, testing, and documentation using                 
              the specifications provided by NSF.                                            
                                                                                             
                   b.  Provide programming, testing, and documentation for                   
              modifications resulting from beta testing of the software at                   
              several academic institutions.                                                 
                                                                                             
                   C.  Determine the optimal compilers for UNIX and PC                       
              environments.                                                                  
                                                                                             
              The award of a T&M Task Order to Compuware in the amount of                    
              $8,280 for Phase One of the Electronic Proposal Submission                     
              system is recommended.
