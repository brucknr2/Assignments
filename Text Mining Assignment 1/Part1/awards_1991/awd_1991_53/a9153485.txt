Abstract    :
                                                                                             
              This award provides support to New Mexico Highlands University                 
              to further develop and design a Comprehensive Regional Center                  
              for Minorities that will stimulate regional change in the                      
              quality of the precollege experiences and preparation of                       
              minority students in science, mathematics and engineering.  The                
              New Mexico Alliance for Science Education (NMASE) is made up of                
              five institutions of higher education and Los Alamos National                  
              Laboratory.
