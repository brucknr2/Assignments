              A theoretical and numerical study of subduction at the base of the             
              mixed layer will be conducted in the north Atlantic ocean. This                
              study will explore the importance of thermodynamic forcing and the             
              effectiveness of the thermocline ventilation in the presence of a              
              mixed layer seasonal cycle.
