Abstract    :
                                                                                             
              The University of Missouri @ Rolla will initiate a four-week,                  
              residential Young Scholars project in Engineering and Earth Science            
              for 20 students entering grade 12.                                             
                                                                                             
              Approximately 120 high school seniors will attend a one-week                   
              program (40 students/week for three consecutive weeks in June)                 
              designed to present science and engineering careers relating to                
              minerals, materials, energy and the environment.  Twenty                       
              participants from this group will be selected to return for an                 
              additional three-week research experience; the particular                      
              discipline of the research will be selected to match student                   
              interest.
