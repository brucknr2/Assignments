Abstract    :
                Computational problems in network design and analysis are  addressed.  Since
              standard approaches to finding exact solutions to  these problems are time
              consuming, the focus is on the  development of new approximation and parallel
              algorithms.    Multi-commodity flow is used as a tool to formulate and solve
              network  problems.
