Abstract    :
               Efficient serial and parallel algorithms, backed by a   mathematical
              programming theory, are developed.  Emphasis is  placed on effective
              distribution of constraints among   processors as well as line-search
              stabilization techniques   nonlinear complementarity and other problems. 
              Finite   termination of  algorithms via weak sharp minima and related concepts
              will   investigated.
