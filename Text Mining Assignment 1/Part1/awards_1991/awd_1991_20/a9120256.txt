Abstract    :
                                                                                             
              This project will support the air travel of a small number of US               
              participants in the XXV International Congress of Psychology to                
              be held in Brussels, Belgium, July 19-24, 1992.  The congress is               
              the quadrennial meeting of the International Union of                          
              Psychological Sciences (IUPsyS) of ICSU.  Arranged by the Belgian              
              Psychological Society, the meeting provides the major opportunity              
              for international scientific exchange among the world's leading                
              psychological scientists, and for young scholars to interact with              
              leading scientists from around the world.
