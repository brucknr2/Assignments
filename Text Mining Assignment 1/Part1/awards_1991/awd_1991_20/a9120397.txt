Abstract    :
              A cooperative                                                                  
              translation/publication project                                                
              will be undertaken on the Report on                                            
              the Great Tangshan Earthquake.                                                 
              This chinese language report was                                               
              prepared by the Institute of                                                   
              Engineering Mechanics in Harbin,                                               
              China which is the major research                                              
              institute on earthquake engineering                                            
              in China.  This magnitude 7.8                                                  
              earthquake leveled the city of                                                 
              Tangshan and killed 243,000 people,                                            
              and industries and city lifelines                                              
              suffered major disruption.  The                                                
              city was composed mainly of                                                    
              structures that had not been                                                   
              designed for earthquake forces and                                             
              approximately 80% of the buildings                                             
              collapsed entirely or in part.                                                 
              Similar vulnerable cities exist in                                             
              the U.S. and also in Central and                                               
              South America, Italy, Greece,                                                  
              Turkey, Pakistan, India, and                                                   
              Indonesia.  When translated into                                               
              english, the report on the Tangshan                                            
              earthquake would be valuable to                                                
              U.S. engineers, architects, and                                                
              government officials in reducing                                               
              seismic vulnerability, and in                                                  
              addition would be valuable to many                                             
              developing countries.                                                          
                                                                                             
              The relevance of the report remains                                            
              high because the vulnerability of                                              
              most cities remains high.  It is                                               
              the purpose of the translation                                                 
              project to make available to U.S.                                              
              readers the information contained                                              
              in the report and thus to encourage                                            
              hazard mitigation measures.
