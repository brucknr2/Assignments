Abstract    :
              This award supports the Fifth International Conference on the                  
              Learning Sciences (formerly the International Conference on                    
              Artificial Intelligence and Education).  The Conference will be                
              held August 4-7, 1991 at The Institute for the Learning Sciences               
              at Northwestern University, Evanston, Illinois and will continue               
              to explore new ideas and tools to improve teaching and learning                
              in all settings (schools, corporate training programs, etc.)  The              
              Conference takes place biennially with alternating American and                
              international hosts.
