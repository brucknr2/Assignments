Abstract    :
              The proposal is to fund 10                                                     
              undergraduate engineering students                                             
              yearly to design aids for specific                                             
              disabled individuals.  The grant                                               
              will expose students to a rewarding                                            
              real-world design experience, and                                              
              should motivate them to choose a                                               
              career in engineering design and                                               
              research to aid the disabled.
