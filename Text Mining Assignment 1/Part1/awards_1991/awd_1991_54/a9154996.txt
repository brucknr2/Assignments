Abstract    :
                                                                                             
              Clarkson University will initiate a five-week, residential,                    
              multidisciplinary Young Scholars project for 25 students entering              
              grades 11 and 12.                                                              
                                                                                             
              Participants will work with faculty research mentors on problems               
              which are part of current research activities, becoming an integral            
              member of the research team.  Faculty will be included from various            
              departments including Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics and Computer             
              Science, Physics and Psychology.  The program will include                     
              lectures, workshops and seminars on the following:  The Nature of              
              Scientific Investigation and Research, Laboratory Safety,                      
              Presentation of Scientific Results, Personal Exploration, Career               
              Development and Scientific Ethics.
