Abstract    :
              This is a proposal to support a                                                
              Colloquium on Cellular and Tissue                                              
              Engineering at the upcoming fall                                               
              meeting of the Biomedical                                                      
              Engineering Society.  The meeting                                              
              will be held on the University of                                              
              Virginia campus in Charlottesville                                             
              on October 12-14, 1991.  The                                                   
              research area of the Colloquium is                                             
              developing rapidly, it presents new                                            
              engineering challenges, and it has                                             
              the potential of achieving                                                     
              significant impact through the                                                 
              development of better health care                                              
              technology.  The area is also                                                  
              promising in the opening up of new                                             
              avenues for potential commercial                                               
              developments.
